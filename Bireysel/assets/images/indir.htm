Our Members

Search for organisations and companies that are part of a global network of GSMA members. Use the filterable list to search by membership type. 

Operator membership

Open to licensed mobile network operators, satellite operators, aircraft operators, maritime operators and telecommunications administrative/ regulatory bodies using a GSM family technology. 

Industry membership

Open to companies in the broader mobile ecosystem, including equipment vendors, device manufacturers and software companies (as well as organisations​ using mobile connectivity ​to create better services).​ 

Rapporteur membership

Open to non-GSM licensed operators moving to LTE/HSPA, fixed wireless/wireless local loop operators, M2M operators, mobile virtual network operators and wholesale network operators. 

Sector membership

Open to other sectors from key adjacent ecosystems such as financial services, manufacturing, aviation, gaming, VoIP, automotive and tower and fibre infrastructure.
